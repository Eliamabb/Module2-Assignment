Accueil

Penguins are a group of aquatic flightless birds. They live almost exclusively in the southern hemisphere: only one
species, the Galápagos penguin, is found north of the Equator. Highly adapted for life in the water, penguins have
countershaded dark and white plumage and flippers for swimming.
